Welcome to the Travel Crown Help Page! Follow the steps below to book your flights, trains, and buses easily.

Booking Flights

  1. Go to the Flights section on our homepage.
  2. Enter your departure and arrival cities.
  3. Select your travel dates and the number of passengers.
  4. Click on "Search Flights".
  5. Review the available options and select your preferred flight.
  6. Provide passenger details and proceed to payment.
  7. Confirm your booking and check your email for the ticket.

Booking Trains

  1. Navigate to the Trains section.
  2. Input your departure and destination stations.
  3. Select your travel date.
  4. Click on "Search Trains".
  5. Choose from the listed train options.
  6. Enter passenger information and select your seating preference.
  7. Complete the payment and receive your e-ticket via email.

Booking Buses

  1. Visit the Buses section of the website.
  2. Fill in your departure and arrival locations.
  3. Select your travel date.
  4. Click "Find Buses".
  5. Browse the available bus services and select your desired option.
  6. Provide necessary passenger details and proceed to checkout.
  7. After payment, your ticket will be emailed to you.
